# Privacy Policy for Autom8ed TOTP Manager

**Effective Date:** May 5, 2026

## 1. Introduction
Autom8ed TOTP Manager ("the Extension") is committed to providing a secure and private two-factor authentication (2FA) experience. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data.

## 2. Data Collection and Usage
**The Extension does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to external servers.**
- **TOTP Secrets:** All secrets, keys, and account labels you enter are stored exclusively on your local device.
- **Encryption:** Data is encrypted at rest using industry-standard **AES-GCM (256-bit)** encryption. The encryption keys are derived locally from your user-provided Master Password using **PBKDF2**.
- **Usage Data:** We do not track your usage, the sites you visit, or when you generate codes.

## 3. Storage
Your data is stored using the `chrome.storage.local` API provided by your browser. This data remains on your machine and is never shared with the developer or any third party.

## 4. No Third-Party Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties. Since we do not collect your data, there is no data to share.

## 5. Chrome Web Store User Data Policy (Limited Use)
Autom8ed TOTP Manager's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs to any other app will adhere to the [Chrome Web Store User Data Policy](https://developer.chrome.com/docs/webstore/user_data/), including the Limited Use requirements.

## 6. Your Control
You have full control over your data. You can export your vault at any time or delete the extension to remove all locally stored data.
